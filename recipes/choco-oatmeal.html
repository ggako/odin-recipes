Chocolate Oatmeal

from Purely Kaylie

Description

Quick and easy recipe. Hit two birds with one stone, chocolate plus the oat goodness.

This recipe is vegan, gluten and dairy-free.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (e.g. almond milk)
  • 1 cup water
  • 2-3 tablespoon cocoa powder
  • 1-2 tablespoon honey (or maple syrup or coconut sugar)
  • 1/2 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt (optional)

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in a small pot
  2. Transfer pot to a stove. Cook over medium heat (for 6-8 minutes), stirring frequently (to avoid oatmeal sticking to bottom of the pot) until thick and creamy consistency
  3. Transfer oatmeal to bowl. Add your favorite topping.
